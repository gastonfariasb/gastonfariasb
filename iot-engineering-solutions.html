In Every Step:

Prototyope x1

The faster you work, the more you can iterate, the better your final product will be. Our team executes over development kits and open source hardware for fast proof of concepts. Get ideas while we deliver products.

Pilot x100

The next step is creating your own circuit board. We continue designing custom boards that to be mounted into Open Hardware and Development Kits. Assembled by hand and 100% tested its is the ideal step to fully understand the posible shapes, ideal functionality and complexities your product design will need to overcome.

​Small Batch x1.000

As you grow from hundreds of units to thousands, automation will provide your next big optimization. You'll switch from boards that are hand-soldered to ones that are assembled by pick-and-place machines. We help you along the way by ensuring quality, ease of manufacturing and costs.

First Run x10.000

Design for Manufacture (DFM) is the next hurdle. The development boards are gone, you have cut your Bill of Materials in half, all while miniaturizing your design. 

​Production x50.000

When you reach full-scale production, optimizing your Bill of Materials becomes extremely important. At this point, we're out of your supply chain. Purchase your hardware directly from our semiconductor partners, and pay us separately for the cloud platform.

​Scale x100.000

At 100,000 units or more, you may want your own Private Cloud. We can host a private instance of our cloud platform that is identical to our multi-tenant platform; the only difference is that it belongs entirely to you. Contact us for more information.
