About US

OUR HISTORY

Created in 1977, DGT Electrónica built and developed, the first completely electronic Private Automatic Branch Exchanger in Latin America. 

30 years later and over 90 developments, DGT reinvented itself and Patagonic Labs was born.

Patagonic Labs develops products for the world from Latin America.

We design M2M products to help companies further control and manage their assets Automagically.
